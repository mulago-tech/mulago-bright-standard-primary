About Us

Mulago Bright Standard Primary School is a caring and disciplined learning environment dedicated to academic excellence, moral development, and holistic growth. Founded in 2010, we provide quality UPE-standard education with optional safe boarding for pupils who need it.

Our mission is to build confident, kind, and capable learners ready for secondary education and life.

Our Curriculum

We follow the Ugandan National Curriculum (UPE) with a focus on strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, science, and values. We also emphasize creativity, music, and physical education.

Admissions

We accept new pupils from P.1 to P.7. Both day and boarding options are available. Admissions are open year-round, with new terms starting in January, May, and September.

Documents Required: Birth certificate, transfer letter (if applicable), 4 passport photos, parent ID.
